All about me

As a GIS Specialist working in a corporate setting, I am continually inspired by the immense potential for innovation within the field of geospatial technology. One of the aspects that excites me most about GIS is the opportunity to explore new ways of optimizing workflows and creating automated solutions. I am passionate about developing custom tools that streamline processes, improve efficiency, and ultimately make a meaningful impact. By combining my expertise in GIS with web development, I am able to contribute to the broader world of spatial data and help shape the future of data management and analysis.

what am i most of the time

Geospatial Tech

I specialize in spatial data analysis, mapping, and web mapping solutions using tools like ArcGIS Pro,AGOL, QGIS, PostgreSQL, and PostGIS. My expertise extends to cartography, Python scripting for automation, and leveraging technologies like ArcGIS Server and OSGeo to streamline workflow processes and create data-driven solutions for informed decision-making.

Developer

As a developer, I combine GIS expertise with web development to create dynamic and interactive geospatial applications. Utilizing technologies such as JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Python, I design custom web mapping solutions and automate processes that integrate spatial data seamlessly into user-friendly interfaces for both desktop and mobile platforms.

Data Analytics

Integrating Microsoft apps such as Excel, SharePoint, Power Apps, Power Automate, and Azure to establish centralized department-wide repositories and streamline analytics workflows. I leverage tools like Power BI and Tableau for data visualization and reporting, while utilizing Visual Basic to automate processes, ensuring efficient and scalable data management across the organization.

Drawing Softwares

I am also well-versed in various drawing software tools, including Autodesk Civil 3D, CAD, and Map, which help me in creating detailed technical designs and maps. In addition to these specialized programs, I also use creative software like SketchUp, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects to produce high-quality graphics and visual content. Drawing is not just part of my profession, but also a personal hobby that I enjoy during my free time, especially using Photoshop to create digital art and experiment with different creative techniques.
